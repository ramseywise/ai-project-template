# How do I descale the machine?

Descale every 2-3 months, or when the descale light turns on. Fill the water tank
with a 1:4 mixture of descaling solution and water. Place a container under the
spout, then run the descale cycle from the settings menu. The machine will run two
rinse cycles automatically after descaling completes. Never use vinegar — it can
damage the internal seals.
