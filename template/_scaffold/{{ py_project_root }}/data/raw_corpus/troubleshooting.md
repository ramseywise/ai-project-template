# The machine won't turn on — what should I check?

First, confirm the power cable is fully seated at both the outlet and the machine.
Check that the water tank is inserted correctly — most models have a safety switch
that blocks power-on if the tank isn't seated. If the machine has a reset button
(usually on the underside), hold it for 5 seconds. If none of this works, the fuse
in the plug may have blown; replace it before contacting support.
